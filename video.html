Kaylee's Work:

Saxophone Studio Recital:


This was my first studio recital on November 15th, 2021 at the Powell Recital Hall in the Martin Center, where I played Sicilliene and Allegro.

Saxophone Ensemble Recital:


This is my first Ensemble Recital with Dr. Loboda's Saxophone Studio. We played many pieces, including Bohemian Rhapsody. It took place on November 22, 2021 in the Powell Recital Hall in the Martin Center.

Opening Fall Bands Concert:


This was my first ever concert at ETSU with the Wind Symphony band. We played pieces such as Steampunk, George Washington Bridge. It starts with concert band at 3:04, and Wind Ensemble at 36:09. Both are well worth a listen!

Ending Fall Bands Concert:


This was my second concert with the Wind ensemble, and it was the Fall Closing Concert. We played Tribute, In Memorium: Kristina for Wind Ensemble, Hymm of St. James, and Christmas with Mozart. Concert Band starts at 3:08, and Wind Ensemble Starts at 38:48.

Opening Spring Semester Bands Concert:


This was my first spring semester concert with the wind ensemble, we played multiple pieces by jack stamp. Concert band starts at 5:16, and wind ensemble starts at 33:16.

Ending Bands Concert:


This is my final spring 2022 semester concert. We played Strange humors, overture to barber of Seville, sea songs, Lincolnshire Posy mvmts 1-6, and fantasy variations. Concert band starts at 3:32, and wind ensemble at 1:02:17.
